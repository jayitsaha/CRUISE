Have a question? Check out the FAQ

Got questions about our cruise trips, packages, or bookings? We've got you covered! Here's everything you need to know.

How do I register for a cruise trip?

To register, simply choose a trip from our available options, select your stateroom, and complete the payment. Each registration generates a unique group ID for managing your booking, which will link all passengers in your group to the same trip.

Can I join multiple cruises at the same time?

No, passengers can only join one cruise at a time. If you wish to take part in multiple trips, you will need to register for each cruise separately, ensuring that dates do not overlap.

What is included in the trip package?

Each cruise package includes all major amenities like stateroom accommodation, meals, and access to various onboard activities. You can enhance your experience with exclusive packages like beverage options, internet, and specialty dining for an additional cost.

How are payments handled for my group?

Payments for a cruise are calculated at the group level. Once you register for a trip, your group will share a single payment for the entire booking, ensuring a smooth transaction for everyone in your party.

What entertainment activities are available onboard?

Onboard entertainment includes a wide variety of options, from theaters and casinos to yoga rooms and night clubs. There are also dedicated spaces for children, as well as pools, saunas, and whirlpools for relaxation.

Can I upgrade my stateroom during my trip?

Yes, upgrades to larger staterooms such as the Club Balcony Suite or The Haven Suite may be available depending on availability. Contact our support team to inquire about upgrade options.
